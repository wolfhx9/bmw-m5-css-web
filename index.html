BMW M5 CS Definition

The BMW M5 CS is the most powerful and fastest version of the M5 series, part of BMW's renowned M lineup known for high performance.
The M5 CS was launched as a limited production special edition for 2022, based on the M5 Competition but with significant enhancements in performance and weight.

BMW M5 CS Features

1. Performance

  • A 4.4-liter twin-turbo V8 engine producing up to 627 horsepower.
  • Acceleration from 0 to 100 km/h in just 2.9 seconds.
  • Electronically limited top speed of 305 km/h.

2. Exterior Design:

  • Lightweight body due to extensive use of carbon fiber in the roof, side mirrors, front and rear diffusers.
  • Gold bronze accents on the front grille, badges, and side mirrors.
  • Lightweight 20-inch wheels with Pirelli P Zero Corsa tires.

3. Interior Design:

  • M Carbon sport seats in the front and rear with special trim.
  • Carbon fiber interior trim and luxurious leather touches.
  • Advanced infotainment system with a touchscreen and high-quality audio system.

4. Handling and Dynamics:

  • M Adaptive suspension system.
  • M xDrive all-wheel-drive system with rear-wheel-drive options.
  • Carbon ceramic brakes for excellent stopping power and reduced unsprung weight.

5. Technology and Safety:

  • Advanced driver assistance system including parking assistance, forward collision warning, and blind-spot monitoring.
  • Advanced BMW Laserlight technology for improved night vision.

The BMW M5 CS combines exceptional performance, luxurious design, and advanced technology, making it one of the most outstanding sports cars in its class.

The driving dynamics and design features of the BMW M5 CS.

The most powerful BMW M5 ever: the BMW M5 CS.
With a reduced weight of 70 kg, it displays top performance and the genetics of the BMW M5 down to the smallest detail.
Its 635 hp of power catapults the high-performance sedan from 0 to 100 km/h in just 3.0 seconds.
The exclusive chassis, lightweight architecture and design components available only for this model give it uniqueness in terms of performance and high-end sporting ambitions.
On the exterior, exclusive styling details such as carbon elements and Goldbronze accents visually and aerodynamically underline its primacy.
Its sporty character continues in the cabin with M sports carbon bucket seats with a marked Nürburgring silhouette at the headrests, contrasting red stitching as well as a rear seat with lightweight 2-seater architecture.

Driving-dynamics

M TwinPower Turbo 8-cylinder petrol engine.

With its 467 kW (635 hp) and 750 Nm torque, the 8-cylinder M TwinPower Turbo petrol engine accelerates from 0 to 100 km/h in 3.0 seconds. The 4.4-liter twin-scroll turbocharged engine, HPI high-precision direct injection and fully variable throttle lift control

Weight reduction.

The intelligent combination of materials with carbon fiber reinforced plastic (CFRP) and special lightweight architecture measures reduce weight while increasing the agility and performance of the BMW M5 CS.

Rot high-gloss carbon-ceramic M brakes.

With the M carbon-ceramic brakes, braking interventions are even more effective. Free from corrosion and showing great resistance to heat and wear, they contribute to weight reduction and thus optimize agility, dynamism and acceleration. This particular technical equipment

M sports exhaust system.

The M Sport exhaust system with sleek twin steel tips emits a characteristic sound which can be modified by an integrated flap system. The SPORT and SPORT+ programs, which can be selected using the driving dynamics button for the engine, are accompanied by an even more intense sound.

The BMW Laserlight shines almost twice as brightly as conventional headlights (up to 650 m) in laser high beam mode. The yellow headlights, the "BMW Laser" inscription on the headlights and the black contours underline the high technicality and sporty character of the vehicle.
